Competition Rules

Entry Requirements

  • Must have a verified TradeMasters account
  • Entry fee of ₹70 per competition
  • Payment must be completed before competition start

Competition Duration

  • 14-day trading period
  • Starts when admin initiates the competition
  • Automatic countdown timer
  • No trading after competition ends

Trading Rules

  • All traders start with ₹10,000 virtual money
  • Can trade all three available products
  • No leverage or short selling
  • Prices update every 10 seconds

Winner Determination

  • Ranked by total portfolio value at competition end
  • Portfolio value = cash + value of all holdings
  • Leaderboard updates every 30 seconds
  • Top 3 traders win cash prizes

Prize Distribution

  • 70% of entry fees form the prize pool
  • 1st Place: 50% of prize pool
  • 2nd Place: 30% of prize pool
  • 3rd Place: 20% of prize pool
  • Prizes distributed within 7 days of competition end

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real money trading?

No, this is virtual trading with simulated money. The competition entry fees and prizes are real, but all trading is done with virtual currency.

How are prices determined?

Prices are simulated with random fluctuations based on each product's volatility and trend parameters. This creates realistic market-like behavior.

Can I withdraw my virtual money?

Virtual money cannot be withdrawn. It's for simulation purposes only. Only competition prize money can be withdrawn.

What happens if I lose all my money?

In the free simulator, you can reset your account. In competitions, if your portfolio reaches zero, you're eliminated from that competition.

How do I claim my prize?

Winners will be contacted via email within 7 days of competition end with prize claim instructions.
